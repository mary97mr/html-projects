Keisuke Baji

First Division Captain of the Tokyo Manji Gang

Here's a time line of Keisuke Baji life

  • 1990 - Born in Shibuya, Tokyo.
  • 2002 - Baji meet Mikey, and with him and other friends from high school Toman is founded.
  • 2003 - Baji and Kazutora wanted to steal a motorcycle for his friend Mikey. But the owner of the shop they wanted to steal is Mikey's brother. Kazutora kills Mikey's bother without knowing this.
  • 2004 - Baji and Kazutora go to a reforatory, Mikey forgives Baji after explaining what happened.
  • 2005 - Dies at the age of 15.

" Pah-chin, Mitsuya, Draken, Mikey, Kazutora. Toman's founding members are my treasure. I wanted to fix this on my own. But, I just couldn't. I died on my own."
